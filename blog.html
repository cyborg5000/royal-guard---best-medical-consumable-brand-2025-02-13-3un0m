The Evolution of Medical Gloves: From Latex to PU Technology

The medical glove industry has evolved dramatically from its latex origins to innovative polyurethane (PU) technology. This transformation addresses historical challenges like latex allergies while offering superior protection and functionality. Modern PU gloves provide enhanced tactile sensitivity, chemical resistance, and pathogen protection without allergy risks. They've gained widespread adoption across surgical procedures, laboratory work, and emergency services. Meeting international safety standards, PU gloves also demonstrate environmental benefits through reduced material usage and improved biodegradability. While initially costlier than traditional options, their durability and performance advantages often result in long-term savings. Ongoing research continues to advance antimicrobial properties and comfort features.
