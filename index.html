About Royal Guard

Founded in 2015, Royal Guard emerged from a vision to revolutionize medical protection equipment. Our journey began when our founders, experienced healthcare professionals, identified critical gaps in existing PPE solutions. Through years of research and development, we pioneered our signature PU glove technology, setting new industry standards for protection and comfort.

Today, Royal Guard stands committed to our mission of safeguarding healthcare heroes worldwide through innovative, sustainable, and accessible protection solutions. Our core values of excellence, innovation, and responsibility guide every aspect of our operations, from production to customer service. We believe in creating products that not only protect lives but also protect our planet for future generations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What certifications do Royal Guard products have?

Our products are FDA-approved and CE-certified, meeting ISO 13485 standards for medical devices. We also maintain certifications for environmental management (ISO 14001) and quality management (ISO 9001).

What sizes are available for your gloves?

Our gloves come in XS, S, M, L, and XL sizes. We also offer custom sizing for bulk orders to ensure perfect fit for your team.

What is your minimum order quantity?

We offer flexible ordering options starting from 100 pieces for small clinics, up to bulk orders for large healthcare facilities. Volume discounts are available for orders over 10,000 pieces.

What is your delivery timeframe?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days within the country. We also offer express shipping options and maintain emergency stock for urgent requirements.

Do you offer samples before bulk purchase?

Yes, we provide sample kits to qualified healthcare facilities. Contact our sales team to request a sample pack containing various sizes and specifications.
